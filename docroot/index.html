About Us

The Snow Devils welcomes all people interested in skiing and snowboarding. People who are new to winter sports have the opportunity to be taught by other members including some members in ski patrol, while advanced skiers and snowboarders will enjoy the numerous trips the club takes during the season. Our weekend trips...

Big Trips

Every break we take big trips to resorts such as Big Bear, Taos, Wolf Creek.

Weekend Trips

During the semester our members take weekend trips to our favorite Arizona resorts Snowbowl and Sunrise.
