Welcome To Snow Devils!!!

Arizona State University's Premier Ski & Boarding Club

Members including both undergad and grad students

About Us

Snow Devils is an active skiing/snowboarding club that welcomes beginners all the way to experts! The club offers the lowest price on the IKON pass that is available anywhere (up to 40% OFF)! Becoming a Snow Devil will introduce you to many other riders and help you improve your skiing and boarding. The club tries its best to make the trips as cost effective as possible. Whether you're a boarder who needs to shred the whole day, or a laid back skier who just needs a few runs, sign on up because deep down we all just want some fresh pow!

Big Trips

We go on about 5 trips every winter season to resorts like Mammoth, Snowbird, Taos, Wolf Creek, and more! Trips tend to be anywhere from $400-$600, or even cheaper if you have the IKON pass. The cost includes the gas to drive up, a place to stay, and your lift tickets for three full days of skiing.

Weekend Trips

During the semester our members take weekend trips to our favorite Arizona resort Snowbowl!

Social Events

Snow Devils doesn't just operate in winter. We have social events all the time, so pay attention to our Instagram and Slack to hear the details!

Free Transportation

Transportation is covered for all members to any club trips.

Membership Benefits

Being a member grants you access to all of our club trips and socials! Our memberships last a whole year and will give you access to the following: club trips, lots of ski related discounts such as the IKON pass, club social, and help with gas costs when going up to the mountain with members!

Reduced Gear Rentals

Snow Devils has a great relationship with the local Ski Pro and members can get 35% off ski/board related rentals!

Access to all Club Trips

Being a member grants you access to all of our club trips and socials!

Free Merch

Becoming a Snow Devil entitles you to an awesome club shirt!

Reduced Lodging and Lift Tickets

Since we are such a large group we can get great prices for lift tickets and lodging!
